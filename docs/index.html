Foundations of Trustworthy AI

Welcome

This topic covers the dimensions of Trustworthy AI: (i) Explainability, (ii) Safety, (iii) Fairness, (iv) Accountability and Reproducibility, (v) Privacy, and (vi) Sustainability.

About

This MOOC is based on the first topic proposed in the TAILOR PhD curriculum about Integrating Approaches for Trustworthy AI. The idea is to adapt the content of The TAILOR Handbook of Trustworthy AI and create a course with six subtopics

  1. Explainable AI Systems
  2. Safety and Robustness
  3. Fairness, Equity, and Justice by Design
  4. Accountability and Reproducibility
  5. Respect for Privacy
  6. Sustainability
